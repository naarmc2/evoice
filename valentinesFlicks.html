Out of Milla's Morning Mind:
Valentine's Day Flicks



Hi Spencies! Valentine's Day is less than one month away and it is never too early to start watching some of these cozy classics. A few of my favorites -

  •  Valentine's Day: Set in LA with Jamie Foxx, Bradley Cooper, Julia Roberts, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Alba, Patrick Dempsey, Jennifer Garner, Ashton Kutcher, Queen Latifah, Taylor Swift, and Taylor Lautner
  •  Titanic: Beautiful costumes and set design with Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet
  •  When Harry Met Sally: Examines if men and women can just be friends with Meg Ryan and Billy Crystal
  •  The Kissing Booth: Confronting hidden crushes with Joey King and Jacob Elordi
  •  Clueless: Beverly Hills teen falls in love with Stacy Dash, Alicia Silverstone, Paul Rudd, Donald Faison, and Brittany Murphy
  •  To All the Boys I've Loved Before: Love letter chaos with Lana Condor, Noah Centineo, and Janel Parrish
  •  50 First Dates: Memory loss and dreams with Adam Sandler and Drew Barrymore
  •  Set it Up: Assistants engage in match-making with Taye Diggs, Lucy Liu, Zoey Deutch, Glen Powell, and Pete Davidson
  •  He's Just Not That Into You: Baltimore story with Bradley Cooper, Jennifer Aniston, Ben Affleck, Ginnifer Goodwin, Jennifer Connelly, Kevin Connolly, Scarlett Johansson, Justin Long, and Drew Barrymore
  •  Crazy Rich Asians: Details love in Singapore with Constance Wu, Henry Golding, Michelle Yeoh, Gemma Chan, and Awkwafina
  •  High School Musical: Dancing into romance with Zac Efron, Vanessa Hudgens, Corbin Bleu, Ashley Tinsdale, Lucas Grabeel, and Monique Coleman
  •  Love and Basketball: Basketball through the ages with Sanaa Lathan, Omar Epps, and Glenndon Chatma
  •  13 Going on 30: One wish gone wrong with Jennifer Garner and Mark Ruffalo
  •  13 Going on 30: Rekindled romance with Ali Wong and Randall Park
  •  How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days: A tough bet leads to love with Matthew McConaughey and Kate Hudson
  •  Falling for Christmas: Amnesia from a skiing accident causes disruption with Lindsay Lohan, Chord Overstreet, Iris Caldwell, and Jack Wagner

- Milla Duca '25
