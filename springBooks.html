Spring Break Books



Hi Spencies! Spring break is right around the corner so now is the perfect time to line up your vacation reads. A few ideas for your bookshelf:

The Great Gatsby” by F. Scott Fitzgerald - Riveting, classic novel set in the Roaring Twenties about wealth, excess, greed, and love. It is an interesting read about a man, Jay Gatsby, who chases his dreams too far in New York.

It Ends With Us” by Colleen Hoover - Emotional read about complicated love and romance. When you are finished with this book you can move on to book two in the series entitled “It Start With Us”.

Two Nights in Lisbon” by Chris Pavone - Thrilling and suspenseful novel about a desperate woman under a time crunch. And, honestly, what reader wouldn’t enjoy the fascinating Lisbon city setting?

Malibu Rising” by Taylor Jenkins Reid - Irresistible book about intimate details and secrets surrounding a prominent family that comes to surface after a wild party in Malibu. This novel which is set in 1983 is complete with a cast of intriguing people including a talented surfer, supermodel, photographer, tennis player, and singer.

Have a wonderful break!

- Milla Duca '25
